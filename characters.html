Cuphead

Cuphead is the titular protagonist of Cuphead. Cuphead is Mugman's irresponsible older twin brother and, like the rest of his family, lives on Inkwell Isle.

Mugman

Mugman is the deuteragonist of Cuphead. He is Cuphead's sensible younger twin brother and, like the rest of his family, lives on Inkwell Isle.

Ms.Chalice

Ms. Chalice is the tritagonist of Cuphead. She was first revealed in the announcement trailer for the DLC, Cuphead: The Delicious Last Course.

The Devil

The Devil is the main antagonist and the final boss of Cuphead: 'Don't Deal With the Devil'. As ruler of the underground isle of Inkwell Hell, he resides within the colossal, castle-like casino that takes up most of the isle's landscape.

King Dice

King Dice is the right-hand man of the Devil and the secondary antagonist of Cuphead, making his very first appearance in the game in the opening cutscene.

Elder kettle

Elder Kettle is a supporting character in Cuphead who acts as the caretaker of Cuphead and Mugman.

Cagney Carnation

Cagney Carnation is the boss of the level titled Floral Fury. He is one of the bosses that can be encountered in Inkwell Isle One.

Ribby and Croaks

Ribby and Croaks are a boss duo in Cuphead. They are fought and encountered in the level Clip Joint Calamity on Inkwell Isle One.

Goopy le Grande

Goopy Le Grande is one of the first bosses in Cuphead. He appears in Ruse Of An Ooze from Inkwell Isle One.

Cala Maria

Cala Maria is one of the bosses on Inkwell Isle Three on the boss level, "High Seas Hi-Jinx!" Her stage is a side-scrolling shooter level.

Baroness Von Bon

Baroness Von Bon Bon is a character of Cuphead residing in Inkwell Isle Two, and the main boss of the Sugarland Shimmy level with her candy-based, anthropomorphic castle fortress Whippet Creampup.

The Root Pack

The Root Pack is a group of four root vegetables residing on Inkwell Isle One, and the boss of the Botanic Panic! level.
